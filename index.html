UO Solar kit

Solar Kit Education and Curriculum

The UO Solar Science Kit has everything young scientists need to measure, investigate, compare, and evaluate the very nature of solar electricity. It’s all here, in an easy-to-store box.

100% Classroom (and Home School) Ready. Lesson Plans Included!

Each kit comes with ready-for-the-classroom, free-to-use lesson plans and access to more online. We believe in education just as much as you do, which is why these plans make it easy to teach the transformation of sunlight to electricity using photovoltaic (PV) cells and scientific principles.

STEM and Beyond

The UO Solar Science Kit supports current educational initiatives to bring more science to the classroom. Lesson plans are designed to encourage investigative, independent learning, and experimentation.

  • Lighting the Way for Young Scientists

  • Helping Students Problem Solve

  • Devel

  • Oping Scientific Minds

  • Investigating the Power of the Sun

These kits provide a wonderful, hands-on opportunity for my students to explore and understand how electricity is made and used.

STEM

STEM Discoveries for K-12 to College

Your students deserve a hands-on learning experience. The UO Solar Science Kit was designed to show how electricity is created from light. Concepts range from connecting and analyzing to more advanced research.

  • Affecting Power Output with Light Color and Intensity

  • Connecting Individual Cells for Required Voltage and Current

  • Conducting Qualitative Observation Experiments

  • Demonstrating the Similarity of PV Cells and Batteries

  • Comparing Spectral Output of Florescent and Incandescent Lights
